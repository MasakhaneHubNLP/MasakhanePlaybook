# Voice Recording

This section covers everything you need to know about voice recording for African language datasets.

## Overview

Voice recording is a crucial step in building speech datasets for African languages. Quality recordings form the foundation for reliable speech recognition and text-to-speech systems.

## Best Practices

- Use high-quality recording equipment
- Ensure quiet recording environments
- Maintain consistent audio formats
- Consider dialect variations
