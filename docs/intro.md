# About Masakhane

Welcome to **Masakhane Playbook** - your comprehensive guide to African language machine translation.

**Masakhane** is a grassroots organization whose mission is to strengthen and spur NLP research in African languages, for Africans, by Africans. The name "Masakhane" means "we build together" in isiZulu.

### Our Mission

- **Democratize** machine translation for African languages
- **Build** community-driven NLP resources
- **Foster** collaboration between researchers across Africa
- **Create** tools and datasets for underrepresented languages

Africa is home to over 2,000 languages, yet most are underrepresented in NLP research and technology. Masakhane works to bridge this gap by creating high-quality translation datasets, developing machine translation models, building community capacity, and promoting research collaboration.

## Getting Started

This playbook will guide you through:

- Understanding machine translation fundamentals
- Working with African language datasets
- Training and evaluating MT models
- Contributing to the Masakhane community

Join us in building technology that serves all African languages and communities.

Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/masakhane-io) to explore our projects and contribute to the community.
